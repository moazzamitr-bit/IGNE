# Content Model

## Publication

Includes slug, locale, title, excerpt, type, category, author, publish date, image, optional PDF, featured flag, tags, and demo marker.

## Event

Includes slug, locale, title, description, date, time, location, format, speakers, image, registration status, capacity, registration URL, report URL, gallery, files, and demo marker.

## News

Includes slug, locale, title, excerpt, category, publish date, image, and demo marker.

## Project

Includes slug, locale, title, excerpt, category, status, and demo marker.

## Research Area

Research areas are shared across locales with localized title, excerpt, topics, and a controlled icon key.

## Expert

Expert profiles remain empty until real verified names, titles, biographies, images, publications, and professional links are provided.

## Translation Structure

Localized route roots are `/fa` and `/en`. Content records are either locale-specific or localized by field, depending on whether the object identity is shared across languages.
